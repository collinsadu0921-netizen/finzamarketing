# Finza SEO — backlink & brand outreach brief

Use this for **directories**, **accountant blogs**, **guest posts**, and **LinkedIn** (educational, not spammy). Always link to the most relevant **hub** + one **blog** article.

## Priority targets (Ghana)

1. **Business directories** — Ghana SME listings, Accra/Tema chambers, software/agritech lists (where allowed).
2. **Accountant & audit firm blogs** — offer a co-authored checklist (VAT lines, client file handover).
3. **Industry newsletters** — logistics, agencies, clinics (invoice + cash-flow angle).
4. **LinkedIn** — short posts linking to `/blog/...` and “search **Finza accounting software Ghana**” where natural.

## Email skeleton (neutral, value-first)

- **Subject:** Quick resource for Ghana SME clients — VAT / invoices / bookkeeping  
- **Body (short):** One paragraph problem (spreadsheet chaos, blended tax lines).  
- **Offer:** Link to one Finza blog article + offer a **guest outline** (800–1,200 words, no hard sell).  
- **CTA:** “Happy to adapt for your readers—no fee.”

## Brand search reinforcement

- Encourage “**Finza accounting software Ghana**” / “**Finza invoicing software Ghana**” in **captions** and **bios** where you control the text (TikTok, LinkedIn, directory profiles)—always with a real URL: `https://www.finza.africa`.

## Weekly rhythm (suggested)

- **3** new or updated internal links on marketing pages (cluster strips help).  
- **3** outreach sends (personalised).  
- **1** LinkedIn educational post pointing at a blog cluster.
